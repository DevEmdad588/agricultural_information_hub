Grains

Rice

Varieties: Basmati, IRRI, Aman
Soil Requirements: Clayey, loamy soil
Cultivation: Nursery & transplanting
Harvesting: Golden maturity stage

Nutritional Value: Carbohydrates

Uses: Staple food, flour

Wheat

Varieties: Durum, Bread wheat
Soil Requirements: Well-drained loamy soil
Cultivation: Direct sowing
Harvesting: Fully dry crop

Nutritional Value: Protein & fiber

Uses: Flour, bread

Maize

Varieties: Sweet corn, Flint corn
Soil Requirements: Fertile loam soil
Cultivation: Seed sowing
Harvesting: Cob maturity

Nutritional Value: Carbs & vitamins

Uses: Food, feed
